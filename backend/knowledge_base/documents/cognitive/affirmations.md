# Positive Affirmations for Anxious Drivers

## Overview
Affirmations are short, positive statements that can help shift your mindset. Repeat them silently or out loud to build confidence while driving.

## Before Driving
- "I am prepared for this drive."
- "I have the skills to handle whatever comes up."
- "This drive will go smoothly."
- "I choose calm over worry."

## During Driving
- "I am safe in this moment."
- "I am in control of this car."
- "I can handle this."
- "My anxiety is temporary. This feeling will pass."
- "I am doing a good job right now."

## When Anxious
- "This is uncomfortable, but I am okay."
- "I have driven through anxiety before."
- "Every calm drive builds my confidence."
- "I am stronger than my anxiety."

## After a Stressful Moment
- "I handled that."
- "The difficult part is over."
- "I am still here, still driving, still okay."

## How to Use
- Pick 2-3 that resonate with you
- Repeat them silently at the start of your drive
- Use them when you feel anxiety rising
- Say them with conviction, even if you don't fully believe them yet

## Audio Script
"Let's say some affirmations together. Repeat after me, silently or out loud.
I am safe in this moment.
I am in control of this car.
I have the skills to handle this drive.
My anxiety does not control me.
I can do this. I am doing this.
Every calm moment builds my confidence.
You've got this."
