# Pre-Drive Preparation Strategies

## Overview
Preparation reduces anxiety. Having a routine before driving helps you feel more in control and ready.

## Physical Preparation

### Comfort Check
- Adjust your seat and mirrors before starting
- Make sure you can reach all controls comfortably
- Ensure good visibility all around
- Set climate control to a comfortable temperature

### Body Ready
- Use the bathroom before leaving
- Avoid too much caffeine
- Have water available
- Eat something if hungry (low blood sugar increases anxiety)

## Mental Preparation

### Route Planning
- Know your route before starting
- Check traffic conditions
- Identify potential stress points
- Have an alternative route in mind

### Mindset
- Take 3 deep breaths before starting the car
- Set a positive intention: "This drive will go well"
- Remind yourself of successful past drives
- Accept that some anxiety is normal and okay

## Environment Setup

### Audio
- Queue up calming music or a podcast
- Have Serene ready to help
- Consider audiobooks for longer drives

### Phone
- Set to Do Not Disturb
- Have navigation ready if needed
- No texting temptations

## Quick Pre-Drive Checklist
1. [ ] Seat and mirrors adjusted
2. [ ] Route reviewed
3. [ ] Phone on Do Not Disturb
4. [ ] Calming audio ready
5. [ ] 3 deep breaths taken
6. [ ] Positive intention set
